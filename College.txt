I made a project which can guide a user get the best college after his jee exam the input is student's jee main/advanced rank his preferred college preferred branch his home state his category and gender and output is how much probability is of his getting into his preffered college and preffered branch and secondly we predict the best college in which he can get into based on his rank and his preferred branch type, the prediction is based on past 6 years data given by josaa in thew website
how we did it 
we took the data in a excel file 1 sheet for each year and only the iits nits and iiits
we separated iits from them coz iit ranks are based on jee adv and nit iiit ranks are based on jee mains 
we cleaned it 
what we did in cleaning part is removed all the ranks that had the letter P in it there were such ranks and made a different dataframes for all the 6 years for both jee advanced and jee mains so in total we had 12 dataframes 2020-2025 data for both the examinations in dataframes 1 dataframe for 1 particular year of one particular exam after getting those two dataframes we made two master dataframes by merging 2025 data with all the other years using left merge keeping 2025 on left so at the end what we had was 2 different master dataframes with columns like Institute,Academic Program Name,Quota,Seat Type,Gender,Closing Rank 2025,Closing Rank 2024,Closing Rank 2023,Closing Rank 2022,Closing Rank 2021,Closing Rank 2020 where the closing rank 2025 was never empty as we deleted all such cases by merging which means we deeted data off all such programs that existed previously but not in 2025, we had both the dataframes in this same schema 1 for jee mains and 1 for jee advanced and also we made a dictionary of all the cities that have iits nits and iiits so that after taking state input from the user we will have his home state colleges and all india quota colleges,
now after cleaning we had processing part
in the processing part we added few more columns in both the dataframes like mean rank, median rank, std deviation, and lower limit and upper limit where lower and upper limit were safe rank range where lower limit= median-std deviation and upper limit=median + std deviation and also a state column in both the dataframes where it will have the state of the college from the dictionary we created earlier this is to check whether that university will be applicable for home state quota or all india quota for the user after adding all this columns using tkinter we made a user interface to get inputs and we took every data from him like his rank, exam, gender, category, state, preffered academic program and preferred college after taking all this input we fetched the data from the data frame like if he chose jee mains we go to jee mains master dataframe ad select the rows where all his other inputs except rank matches and then based on the upper, lower limit of that row and his rank if his lower limit<rank<upper limit we give out put as safe option if his rank<lower limit output as very high chance and if rank>upper limit we give output as risky, high risky and very high risky based on how far his rank is from upper limit wrt std deviation and then second output is a college and a branch combo where the branch will be what his input was but the college will be what we suggest as a safe option for him based on his rank, exam, gender, category, state, preffered academic program and the best will be selected by the lowest upper limit under which his rank falls where the academic program matches his preferred academic program and everything matches like home state quota or all india quota based on his given state and college state 
so these two outputs
1 is risk of getting into the preferred college with preferred branch and other is best option according to us to get into with your preferred branch
